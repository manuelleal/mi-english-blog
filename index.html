Welcome to the Technology Applied to L2 Teaching Blog

This blog, designed by Christiam Puentes, is dedicated to exploring how technology can be effectively integrated into second language (L2) teaching. Here, you'll find various activities and readings aimed at enhancing language learning through technological tools.

Interactive Readings

Technology has transformed language learning by providing access to authentic materials and interactive platforms. Tools such as language learning apps, online courses, and virtual reality environments offer immersive experiences that enhance linguistic skills.

Questions:

  • How has technology changed traditional language learning methods?
  • What are some examples of technological tools used in language learning?
  • How can virtual reality contribute to language acquisition?

Mobile-Assisted Language Learning (MALL) refers to the use of mobile devices to support language learning. Smartphones and tablets enable learners to practice language skills anytime and anywhere, making learning more flexible and personalized.

Questions:

  • What is MALL, and how does it differ from traditional language learning?
  • What are the benefits of using mobile devices for language learning?
  • Can you name some popular language learning apps available on mobile devices?
