Why is English important?

Learning English opens up opportunities for education, career advancement, and cultural exchange. It allows you to connect with people from different parts of the world and access a wealth of knowledge and resources.

Reading 2: Technology in the Classroom

Technology in the Classroom:

The integration of digital tools into language classrooms has revolutionized the way students learn. Interactive technologies such as smartboards, language learning apps, and online platforms have made lessons more engaging and accessible. Teachers can now use multimedia resources to explain complex concepts, while students can practice at their own pace using personalized learning tools.
